The Accounting Officer role is a core position within EP Partners' client accounting team. You will manage the full accounting cycle for a portfolio of corporate clients — from bookkeeping and payroll processing through to financial statement preparation and GDT tax filing support.

You will collaborate with our tax advisors to ensure clients maintain compliant and well-organized financial records, enabling timely and accurate tax filings and audit-ready financials.
