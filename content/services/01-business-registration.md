EP Partners manages the full company formation lifecycle in Cambodia. From selecting the right legal structure — limited liability company, branch office, or representative office — through to post-registration compliance setup, we ensure your business is properly established and ready to operate from day one.

Our trilingual team (English, Khmer, Mandarin) handles all government submissions and coordinates directly with the Ministry of Commerce, General Department of Taxation, and National Social Security Fund on your behalf.
