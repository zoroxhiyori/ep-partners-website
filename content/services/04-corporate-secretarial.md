Maintaining good corporate standing in Cambodia requires ongoing attention to statutory deadlines, document filings, and governance records. EP Partners acts as your corporate secretary, managing all annual renewal obligations, shareholder and director change notifications, and regulatory filings with the Ministry of Commerce.

We also assist with corporate restructuring, share transfers, capital increases, and preparation of shareholder agreements — ensuring your corporate documents accurately reflect your business structure at all times.
