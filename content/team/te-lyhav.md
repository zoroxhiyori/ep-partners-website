Te Lyhav founded EP Partners with a vision to bridge the gap between international business standards and Cambodia's evolving regulatory environment. With over a decade of experience advising multinational corporations, SMEs, and family-owned businesses across the region, Lyhav brings deep expertise in tax structuring, corporate compliance, and cross-border investment advisory.

Prior to founding EP Partners, Lyhav held senior roles at international professional services firms and gained hands-on experience managing complex engagements across Cambodia, China, and Southeast Asia. Fluent in English, Khmer, and Mandarin, he is uniquely positioned to serve clients across the region.
